About Team Cash Flow

Our mission is to help people in our local community create a better future for their families through education, training, and employment opportunities. We have been serving the Lincoln and surrounding areas for 25 years.

The state of Nebraska refers clients to us upon receiving ADC and other benefits. We provide clients with additional assistance to help them meet their life goals. If you have been referred to us, please use your Client ID# to create your account and start your journey today.
